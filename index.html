The future

What's in store for humanity?

The near future is long and daunting. One could argue that humanity no longer has a future considering it's long track record of destruction and war, but this timeline should serve to give an idea of what's to come.

The 2020's

In the 2020's we will be in a period of large scale change. We will reach our tipping point in terms of oil and the economy and the world as we know will be forced to shift towards renewable energy.

Other notable events include:

  • Generation X(1960 - 1980's) reshapes global politics through their more liberal ideals.
  • 2020-2025 - Five-year survival rates for Hodgkin's lymphoma are approaching 100%
  • Internet use reaches 5 billion worldwide due to increase in accessibility and affordability

The 2030's

The 2030's will be a decade of futuristic change. The global population will be reaching it's tipping point with an addition 2 billion people living on earth.

Other notable changes include:

  • Desalination has exploded in use
  • AI is widespread
  • The majority of new vehicles are plug-in electric, or hybrids
  • Emerging job titles of today
    • 'New Science' Ethicist
    • Waste Data Handler
    • Vertical Farmer
    • Nano Medic
    • Virtual Teacher
    • Body Part Maker
